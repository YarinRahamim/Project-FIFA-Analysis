About The Project

Every year FIFA organization choose the best 11 football players of the year.
Our project come to help to solve this problem with predict the players with their yearly football data.
We used a data of almost 18K players, and used some diffrent personality varibels to reduce and getting the players with the mostly chance to be chosen.
After we reduced the data, we analyzed it and got the squad to be with the best 11 players of the year.
